What is tjSTAR?

Every spring, TJHSST sets aside a full school day for seniors to present the research they've been working on all year at the tjSTAR (Thomas Jefferson Symposium to Advance Research).

Each senior gets a time slot and a room. Some present solo, some in teams. The topics come from TJ's 10 research labs + Mentorship: Computer Systems, Neuroscience, Quantum Physics, Oceanography, and more. The work spans across experiments, code, prototypes, and results from university partnerships.
